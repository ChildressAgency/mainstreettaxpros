About Our Firm

Main Street Accounting Services (MSAS) has provided outstanding service to our clients since 1986 because of our dedication to continuing education, professionalism and small town values.

Continuing Education

As Enrolled Agents, tax professional who have passed the IRS's grueling 3 part Special Enrollment Exam, we are required to obtain at least 72 hours of professional education every 3 years to keep our professional designation. This keeps us up to date on tax issues that impact our clients businesses and personal tax planning.

Professionalism

With almost 50 years of combined tax preparation and accounting experience, we are one of the most experienced firms in the area. By combining our expertise, experience and the energy of our staff, each client receives close personal and professional attention.

Our high standards, service and specialized staff spell the difference between our outstanding performance, and other firms. We make sure that every client is served by the expertise of our whole firm.

Small town Values

We live and work in this community. We have to be able to walk down the street and look our neighbors, who in many cases are also clients, in the eye. We also need to look ourselves in the mirror. For those reasons our clients can stop by and ask questions and/or call us on the phone without having to worry about the "clock running" on their bill. We give quality service and charge fair rates for the work we do. That is how we have continued to keep clients coming back AND continue to stay in business.
